Biology

This course will follow Campbell Biology 9th edition by Campbell and Reece , and will be a condensed version of the subject material taught in AP Biology. Topics covered include biochemistry, cells and processes, evolution, microbiology, botany, zoology, and ecology.

Physics

This class will be a condensed version of the AP Physics 2 syllabus. Topics covered include a brief review of Physics 1 topics, rotational motion, electrostatics, electric circuits, electricity and magnetism, optics, fluids, and thermodynamics.

Earth Science

This class will cover key processes and fundamentals of Earth Science. Topics covered include Geology, Oceanology, Meterology, and Astronomy

Chemistry

This class's curriculum is based on the book Chemistry: 10th Edition, by Zumdahl and Zumdahl. Topics covered include Chemical Reactions, Stoichiometry, Nuclear Chemistry, Equilibrium Chemistry, Acids and Buffers, Thermochemistry, the Periodic Table, Bond types, Gas Laws, and Organic Chemistry.
