Outreach

Start Your Own Chapter

One of Science Infinity's goal is to provide education to students all around the World, but to do that we need more science-loving High School Volunteers from around the World to start their own chapters. If you are such a student, and you are interested in learning more about how you can start your own chapter, feel free to send an email to info@scienceinfinity.org.

For all interested chapters, we provide material, possible funding for rent, and anything at all that is needed.


Why You Should Start Your Own Chapter

TThe warm feeling of spreading your knowledge to bright young minds and giving back to your community. Volunteering for a Non-Profit Organization and being able to learn how to effectively lead, market, and manage your own branch. This shows great leadership skills that will be useful in the future. Opportunity to win the President's Volunteer Service Award. A chance to run your branch of a Non-Profit organization and collaborate with others around the country to have a global impact and promote education!


Start Your Own Chapter

Want to join our efforts but not sure where to start?


First start off by emailing info@scienceinfinity.org through the form at the bottom of the page! We will provide you everything that you need to get started from materials to registration forms. We have experience on how to get started so we will help you get students.

Next, talk to your friends, get a group of teachers/volunteers that want to start this branch with you.

Then, find a venue. The venue can be free or paid but you should be able to use it for 2 hours every week or so. If the venues you find need to be rented out, we can provide you the money to do so - at a reasonable price.

Last, Advertise! Go to your former middle schools and tell them about this. Advertise through social media as well! At this point, if you have contacted us and finished the previous steps, we will have created a registration form for your chapter on this website already, and you can send your students here.
