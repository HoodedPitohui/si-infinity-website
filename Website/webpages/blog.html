Daniel Sun places 7th at OPHO (June 10)

Our very own Physics teacher at the Seattle Chapter got 7th at the OHPO competition. He placed 1st out of all the participants in the United States. Everyone at the Science Infinity organization is incredibly proud of him and this incredible achievement! We know there are only bright things to come in Daniel's future.

(Also featured are two of our teachers, Arnav Narula and Raghav Narula and a cameo from Arpit Ranasaria in the back)
