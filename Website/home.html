Science Infinity

Our Mission

Empowering Young Minds

Science Infinity is a High School Volunteer run 501(c)(3) non-profit organization committed to spreading their knowledge to young schoolers (5th grade+) in the fields of Earth Science, Biology, Physics, and Chemistry in order to give them early exposure to the advanced sciences.  The funds raised from donations are used to fund various educational programs closer to home and around the world. Special funds are set aside to help underprivileged students.



Outreach

One of our goals here at Science Infinity is not only to provide education to Seattle-based students, but to provide education around the US and education related aid to the underprivileged and needy around the world. Volunteer
